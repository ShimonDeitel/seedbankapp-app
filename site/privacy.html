Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-08

Seedbank is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage and is never transmitted to us or any third party. We do not collect, track, or sell any personal information.

The only network communication in this app is with Apple's App Store servers for subscription purchases and restores, handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework. We never see your payment details.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is deleted with it.
